Whitewater Rafting Botswana

Our mission is to offer thrilling, safe, and family-friendly rafting experiences in Botswana's stunning waterways. Join us for an unforgettable adventure that combines fun, safety, and breathtaking nature.

History

Founded in 2005, Whitewater Rafting Botswana began as a small team passionate about outdoor adventure. Over the years, we've grown into Botswana's premier rafting company, dedicated to delivering safe, exhilarating, and family-friendly river trips.
